⚠ Beta disclaimer: IV Buddy is in early development and has not been formally validated. It is a reference tool only — always verify with your institution's approved references, manufacturer labeling, and current USP <797> guidelines before preparing or administering any IV medication.

What is IV Buddy?

IV Buddy is a fast, single-purpose reference for IV medication preparation — designed for nurses, pharmacy technicians, and pharmacists who need a quick answer at the Pyxis, the IV room bench, or the bedside. One search, one screen, get back to the patient.

What IV Buddy is NOT

IV Buddy is not a substitute for Lexicomp, Micromedex, Trissel's, King Guide, or your institution's formulary. It does not replace pharmacist verification. It does not account for patient-specific factors, drug interactions, or institutional protocols.

Data sources

Entries are compiled from manufacturer prescribing information, published stability literature, and standard pharmacy references. Individual citations per field are planned for a future version.

Coverage

Current version covers adult IV preparations only. Pediatric concentrations and institutional protocol customization are planned for future tiers.

Found an error?

Clinical accuracy is our top priority. If something looks wrong, please use the "Suggest a Fix" button — every report is reviewed.
